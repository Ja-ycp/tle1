Netiquette Guidelines

Netiquette — or internet etiquette — covers how we should act when communicating online. Whether it's sending an email, joining a video call, or posting in a discussion board, these guidelines help keep online spaces respectful and productive.

  • Rule 1 Treat others the way you want to be treated, even through a screen.
  • Rule 2 Use proper spelling and grammar — it shows respect for the reader.
  • Rule 3 Don't share other people's private information without their permission.
  • Rule 4 Be careful with tone — what seems like a joke in person can come across wrong in text.
  • Rule 5 Give credit where it's due. Cite your sources and don't pass off others' work as your own.
  • Rule 6 Think before you post. What goes online often stays online.

For the full set of guidelines, visit the Netiquette module.
